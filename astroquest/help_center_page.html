Common Questions

How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.
How can I update my account details?
Log in to your account, navigate to "Settings" and select "Account Information" to update your details.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to "Settings" > "Subscription" and click on "Cancel Subscription". Follow the prompts to complete the process.
Where can I download the mobile app?
Our app is available on the App Store for iOS devices and Google Play Store for Android devices.

Troubleshooting

  • App not loading? Clear your browser cache or try using a different browser.
  • Payment issues? Verify your payment method is valid and has sufficient funds.
  • Login problems? Make sure your username and password are correct. Try the password reset option if needed.
  • Error messages? Take a screenshot of the error and include it when contacting our support team.

How to Get Support

We offer multiple support channels to help address your needs:

  • Self-service: Browse our help articles and FAQs
  • Live chat: Available Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm EST
  • Email support: 24/7 with response within 24 hours
  • Phone support: Premium customers only
