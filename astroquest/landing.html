Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about AstroQuest

What is AstroQuest?

+

AstroQuest is an educational platform dedicated to teaching astronomy and astrophysics through interactive lessons, quizzes, and stunning visuals. We make complex cosmic concepts accessible to learners of all ages.

Is AstroQuest suitable for beginners?

+

Absolutely! AstroQuest is designed for space enthusiasts of all knowledge levels. We offer content ranging from beginner-friendly introductions to more advanced topics for those looking to deepen their understanding.

Do I need to sign up to access the content?

+

While some basic content is available to all visitors, creating a free account gives you access to our full library of lessons, interactive quizzes, progress tracking, and personalized learning paths.

Where do the images come from?

+

Our gallery features images from NASA's extensive collection, accessed through their public API. All images are credited appropriately and used for educational purposes.

Can I suggest topics or features?

+

Yes! We love hearing from our community. Please use the contact information in the footer to send us your suggestions, feedback, or questions.
